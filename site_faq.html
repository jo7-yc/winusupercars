Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our competitions, prizes, and how it all works.

PARTICIPATION
How do I enter?
It's simple. Visit our Competitions page, select the supercar you want to win, choose your ticket pack, answer the skill-based quiz question, and complete your secure payment. You're in the draw as soon as your payment is confirmed.
How much does a ticket cost?
Tickets start at just $49 for a 5-ticket pack. We also offer a 15-ticket pack at $129 and a 50-ticket pack at $349. The more tickets you buy, the lower the cost per ticket and the higher your chances of winning.
Can I buy multiple packs?
Yes. You can purchase as many packs as you like, up to a maximum of 50 tickets per person per competition. This limit ensures fairness for all participants.
Is there an age limit?
Yes, you must be at least 18 years old to participate. Age verification may be required before any prize is awarded. You must also reside in a jurisdiction where skill-based competitions are permitted under local law.
DRAW
How is the winner selected?
The winner is selected using a certified third-party Random Number Generator (RNG). Only participants who correctly answered the skill-based quiz question are eligible. The RNG is independently audited and compliant with industry standards to ensure complete impartiality.
When do draws take place?
Each competition has a closing date published on the Competitions page. The draw takes place within 48 hours of the competition closing or once all tickets are sold, whichever comes first. Exact draw dates are announced 7 days in advance via email and on our social media channels.
Is the draw truly random?
Absolutely. Our RNG provider is independently certified and audited. Every eligible ticket has an equal chance of being selected. Draw results are verified by an independent third party and published on our Winners page within 48 hours.
PRIZES
What happens if I win?
You will be contacted within 24 hours of the draw via the email address linked to your account. You'll need to verify your identity with a valid government-issued ID and proof of address. You then choose between receiving the car (Option A) or the cash alternative at 80% of the car's retail value (Option B). Your choice is final and must be communicated within 72 hours.
Is delivery included?
Yes. If you choose Option A, we deliver the supercar to your door anywhere in the continental United States at no extra cost. Title transfer and registration assistance are included. For locations outside the continental US, additional shipping fees may apply.
Do I have to pay taxes?
Yes, the winner is solely responsible for all applicable federal, state, and local taxes on the prize. For US residents, WIN U SUPERCARS will issue a Form 1099-MISC to the IRS for any prize valued over $600. We strongly recommend consulting a tax professional to understand the tax implications of your winnings.
SECURITY & LEGAL
Is WIN U SUPERCARS legal?
Yes. WIN U SUPERCARS LLC is a legally registered company in the State of Florida. Our competitions are structured as skill-based contests, which are legal under federal law and the laws of the State of Florida. Participants must correctly answer a quiz question to be eligible for the draw, which distinguishes our competitions from games of pure chance.
Is my data protected?
Your personal data is encrypted using AES-256 encryption and stored on secure servers. We comply with the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and never sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for commercial purposes. Payments are processed securely through our payment partner.
How do I contact support?
You can reach our support team at contact@winusupercars.com. We respond to all inquiries within 48 business hours. For privacy-related requests, please write to privacy@winusupercars.com.

Still have questions?

Our team is available 7 days a week to help you.
