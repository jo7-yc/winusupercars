Draw Process

WIN U SUPERCARS organizes draws following the close of each competition, at dates communicated in advance on the website and by email notification to registered participants.

The draw is conducted using a certified Random Number Generator (RNG) algorithm, ensuring fairness and transparency in the selection process. This algorithm is developed and audited by an independent technology provider.

Draw results are verified by an independent third party, whose compliance report is made available on the Company's website within forty-eight (48) hours of the draw. Each draw result is final and binding.

Article 06

Winner Selection

The winner is selected at random from among all valid tickets registered for the relevant competition. Only participants who correctly answered the skill-based quiz question are eligible for the draw. Each eligible ticket has an equal chance of being selected.

Notification: The winner is notified within twenty-four (24) hours of the draw, by email to the address associated with their user account and by platform notification.

Identity verification: The winner must provide a valid government-issued photo ID (passport or driver's license) and proof of address dated within the last three (3) months to confirm eligibility.

Response deadline: The winner has seventy-two (72) hours from notification to confirm acceptance of the prize and provide the required documents. If no response is received or documents are non-compliant within this period, the prize is forfeited and a replacement draw is conducted within seven (7) business days.

Article 07

Prize Delivery

The winner may choose between two options for receiving their prize:

Option A — Vehicle:

  • Delivery of the vehicle within seven (7) business days of final verification of the winner's documentation.
  • Title transfer is completed in the winner's name. Transfer fees are borne by WIN U SUPERCARS.
  • Transportation and delivery costs are covered by the Company for deliveries within the continental United States. Additional fees may apply for deliveries outside the continental US.

Option B — Cash alternative:

  • The winner may opt to receive eighty percent (80%) of the estimated retail value of the vehicle at the time of the draw.
  • Payment is made by bank transfer within fourteen (14) business days of receiving the winner's banking details.
  • The choice between Option A and Option B is irrevocable and must be communicated within the seventy-two (72) hour deadline specified in Article 6.
