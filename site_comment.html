Transparency

We Use a Certified Electronic Random Draw System

Our draw process is powered by a third-party certified Random Number Generator (RNG). This system ensures that every participant has an equal and verifiable chance of winning, regardless of when they purchased their ticket or how many they hold.

The RNG is independently audited and compliant with industry standards. Draw results are final, binding, and published publicly on our Winners page within 48 hours of each competition closing.

What Happens When You Win

Every winner gets to choose how they'd like to receive their prize.

Option A
Receive the Car
Your supercar is delivered directly to your door, anywhere in the continental United States. Delivery, registration assistance, and handover are all included at no additional cost.
Option B
Cash Alternative
Prefer cash? You can opt to receive 80% of the car's retail value as a direct bank transfer. Funds are sent within 14 business days of prize acceptance.

Frequently Asked Questions

How are winners selected? +

Winners are selected using a certified third-party Random Number Generator (RNG) that is independently audited. Every ticket has an equal chance of winning.

How many tickets can I buy? +

Each player can purchase up to 50 tickets per competition. This limit ensures fair access for all participants.

Can I choose the cash alternative instead of the car? +

Yes. Every winner can choose to receive 80% of the car's retail value as a direct bank transfer instead of taking delivery of the vehicle.

What happens if not all tickets are sold? +

The competition will still take place on the scheduled date regardless of how many tickets are sold. There will always be a winner.

Is the competition legitimate? +

Absolutely. Our draw system is certified and independently audited. All results are published on our Winners page within 48 hours of each draw.
